The EMI Card is not a credit card. By using this card our customers can
purchase any durable, digital, lifestyle or retail products with minimal documentation.

You need to select "No cost EMI products" while shopping online Steps to buy on website using EMI.

  1. Login or sign up
  2. On the Home Page, search for a product of your choice from the list below.
  3. On the product page, click "Shop Now".
  4. Select EMI from the payment options displayed and click "Buy now".

We would like to inform you that the original invoice copies and loan documents are maintained with us for our records.
You may contact the dealer should you wish to retrieve a copy of the same. Alternatively, follow these steps:
Customer Portal > View loan details > Select view details > Select document vault.

You can apply for an EMI card while visiting our nearest authorized partner store for availing a consumer durable/digital loan. If you are an existing customer with us, you can apply for the card online through the Experia portal, under the offer tab. The EMI card comes to you by making a onetime payment of a nominal fee of Rs. 417. However, if the card remains unutilized for a year from the date of card issuance, there is a yearly fee of Rs. 117 for the non-usage.

You can view your EMI Card limit on the Customer Portal.
Log in to the Customer Portal > Select cards > EMI Card > See details.
